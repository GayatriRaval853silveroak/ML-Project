Dr. Dorota Jaworska

BSc(Hons)Nursing, DIP HE Midwifery, BSCCP, INP

Welcome to my clinic,

I am a fully qualified Adult Nurse, Midwife and Independent Prescriber with over ten years of experience in advanced and autonomous clinical practice within the NHS and private healthcare sector. I am registered with the Nursing and Midwifery Council and a member of the Royal College of Midwives. As a part of my ongoing clinical development, I regularly undertake learning opportunities to ensure safe clinical practice.

Given my advanced medical background and passion for natural beauty, I decided to fund DJ Clinic to offer my clients bespoke, personal and highly ethical treatment. I aim to enhance your natural look, restore confidence in your appearance and boost your self-esteem.


Accomplishing your desired outcome is a gradual process. To enhance and rejuvenate the natural look, I encourage my clients to build their look slowly. I firmly believe that this approach is essential to maintain your natural appearance and avoid risks and complications associated with any treatments. You can be assured of an effective, safe and highly professional service. There is always a review two weeks after injectable treatments to ensure that you are well and satisfied with your treatment outcome.

Our Mission

We aim to be a clinic that combines the best aesthetic and beauty treatment with strong ethical principles and a culture of care and compassion to enrich your natural beauty.

Our Values

Serving patients with quality medical aesthetic care and helping them to feel empowered as individuals by enriching their natural beauty.
